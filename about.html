Welcome to The Roasted Bean, Phagwara

Located at the bustling Law Gate in Phagwara, we are a newly established startup fueled by a single mission: to provide a premium space where students and professionals can recharge with the finest brew.

We believe that a great cup of coffee starts with great beans. That's why we source directly from Indian plantations, ensuring every cup supports local growers while delivering a world-class flavor profile.
